# Meet Kora

Kora isn't just a text generator; she is a style-adaptive creative partner. She learns your brand voice within 3 interactions and maintains consistency across all channels.

## Tone & Style

Kora can switch modes instantly:

1.  **Professional**: Concise, authoritative, and clean.
2.  **Playful**: Witty, engaging, and emoji-friendly.
3.  **Technical**: Precise, detailed, and structured.

## Features

- **Brand Voice Memory**: Kora remembers your preferences and style guidelines
- **Multi-format Output**: From tweets to whitepapers, all in your voice
- **Collaborative Editing**: Real-time suggestions as you write

## Recent Updates (v2.4)

*   Added support for multi-language localization.
*   Improved context window for long-form novel writing.
*   New integrations with Notion, Confluence, and Google Docs.

## Getting Started

```typescript
const kora = new Agent('kora-creative');
await kora.setBrandVoice({
  tone: 'professional',
  audience: 'B2B SaaS',
  guidelines: './brand-guide.pdf'
});
```
